# EcoFlow Delta Pro Ultra

Integrate your **EcoFlow Delta Pro Ultra** battery system into Home Assistant with real-time monitoring and full control — powered by the official EcoFlow Open API via MQTT.

---

## What you get

### 📊 25 Sensors
Real-time monitoring of every power flow:
- **Battery** — State of Charge, capacity, voltage, current, temperature, cycles, remaining time
- **AC** — Input & output power, voltage, frequency, and charge rate
- **Solar** — Input power, voltage, and current (MPPT)
- **DC / USB** — Car port, USB-A, and USB-C output power
- **Grid** — Grid input power
- **Limits** — Charge upper limit, discharge lower limit, backup reserve level

### 🔌 3 Switches
Instant on/off control:
- **AC Output** — Enable/disable the AC inverter
- **DC Output** — Enable/disable the 12 V car port
- **EPS Mode** — Toggle Emergency Power Supply (UPS) mode

### 🎚️ 4 Number Sliders
Fine-tune your battery behaviour:
- AC Charging Power Limit (200–3000 W)
- Backup Reserve Level (0–100%)
- Charge Upper Limit (50–100%)
- Discharge Lower Limit (0–30%)

---

## Setup requirements

1. A free **EcoFlow Developer account** — [developer.ecoflow.com](https://developer.ecoflow.com)
2. An **Access Key** and **Secret Key** from the developer portal
3. Your device **Serial Number (SN)** from the EcoFlow app
